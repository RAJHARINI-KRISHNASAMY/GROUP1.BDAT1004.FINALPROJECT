ANALYSIS OF CARS DATASET

'Car' in a simple term, a four wheeled motor vehicle for travelling from one place to another. Firstly, Modern car came into world by 20th century and was invented by German inventor Karl Benz. The first car came into use was the 1908 "Model T",manufactured by American Manufacturer "Ford Motor Company". Later,Car was regarded as pride factor and was owned by all the big shots. Finally, car became commercially avaliable after 1975 and was widely used for transportation. The prices of car was calculated on the basis of Brand, Fuel Type, Engine Power, etc.

The cars dataset is used to predict the best car manufacturers, on the basis of Mileage, Engine Power, Fuel Type, Auto gear facility, etc. Here in our dataset we have different cars model from 1980 - 2016 listed on different prices and makers, which is taken from the Analytixlabs.From the analysis, we can find the number of cars repeased per year, Top car Brands based on Mileage and CC, Number of cars with different gear type and fuel type.

Questions for the analysis

  1. Which top 5 makers prioritize mileage for their car models?
  2. What was the percentage of car with different fule type between the year 1980-2016?
  3. Which top 5 makers prioritize cc for their cars ?
  4. Which top 5 makers have SUV model car with relatively good mileage?
  5. What was the percentage of car with different Gear type between the year 1980-2016?
